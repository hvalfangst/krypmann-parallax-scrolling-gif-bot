Background for 2d side-scrolling game, which have 4 separate horizontal layers for parallax scrolling. Layer 1: Distant mountain silhouettes with smooth curves, repeating seamlessly. Layer 2: Rolling hills with gentle slopes and sparse trees, tiled horizontally. Layer 3: Closer trees and bushes with varied shapes, creating depth. Layer 4: Foreground grass and small rocks with detailed textures, tile seamlessly for continuous movement.
