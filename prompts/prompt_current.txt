Background for 2d side-scrolling game, which have 4 separate horizontal layers for parallax scrolling. Layer 1: Distant mountain silhouettes with gentle curves, repeating seamlessly. Layer 2: Rolling hills with sparse trees, smoothly tiled. Layer 3: Closer foliage and small rocks, with varied but seamless patterns. Layer 4: Pathway and foreground grass with simple, tileable textures for player interaction.
